Real Estate Property Management Features

Advance Dashboard

Property Stats
  • Total number of properties including available, sold, leased, on sale, and on lease status.
Rent Stats
  • Track draft, running, and expired contracts, total rent collections, and invoice due reminders.
Sale Stats
  • Overview of total booked and sold properties with corresponding sales invoice amounts.
Interactive Graphs
  • Visualize data for property types, status, broker-wise rentals, and invoice status (due vs. paid) for both rent and sales.

Property Management (Single & Multiple)

Main & Sub-Properties
  • Manage multiple units within the same property location using the main and sub-property structure.
Area Measurements
  • Define and calculate property areas (e.g., bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, hall, backyard, parking, etc.).
Amenities, Features & Nearby
  • Add property-specific amenities, unique features, and nearby location connectivity details.
Geo Location Map
  • Open property location on Google Maps using geo-coordinates — no API key required.
Property Status
  • Manage and filter properties by status: Available, Booked, Rented, Sold, Draft, or On Sale/Lease.
Search, Filters & Grouping
  • Advanced search & filters by type, status, landlord, location, and rent/sale. Group by multiple attributes.
Property Types
  • All-in-one property solutions: Residential, Commercial, Industrial, and Land.
Images, Documents, & Floor Plans
  • Upload property images, related documents, and detailed floor plans for better presentation.
Property Brochures
  • Generate professional PDF brochures with complete property information for distribution.
Certificates & Tags
  • Assign custom tags to properties and manage safety certificates and compliance documents.
Views
  • Access property records through various views: Kanban, Tree, Form, Pivot, and Activity View.

Property Booking & Leads Management

Leads Management
  • Ability to create leads based on individual properties within Odoo CRM
  • Smart action button to view all leads related to a specific property
Property Booking Management
  • Book properties for sale or lease through CRM system
  • Manage property bookings with token amount for sales
  • Custom Refund Amount functionality for Property Booking Customers
  • Send email notifications on successful property bookings
  • Manage follow-up activities with potential customers

Property Sales Management

Advance Booking with Deposit & Refund
  • Advance booking is available, and you can pay a deposit to confirm your reservation.
  • Refunds available if you cancel your booking early.
Flexible Payment & Invoice Management
  • Manage full and installment-based payments (Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly) with automated invoicing for property sales.
Automated Sales Contract Notifications
  • Automatically send detailed sales contract reports with PDF attachments via email to customers and internal stakeholders.
Property Selling Contract
  • Generate and manage comprehensive sales contracts with detailed terms, conditions, and downloadable PDF documentation for every property transaction.
Brokerage & Commission Tracking
  • Manage third-party agent commissions using fixed or percentage-based calculations, integrated with your sales flow.

Property Rent or Lease Management

Flexible Rent/Lease Duration
  • Create customizable rent or lease contracts for various durations—daily, monthly, or yearly—based on tenant needs.
Rent Invoice with Installments
  • Generate full rent invoices or configure installment-based payment plans easily.
Early Invoice Scheduling
  • Define how many days in advance to auto-create rent invoices for the next billing cycle.
Online Payment Integration
  • Seamless integration with payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, Alipay, Mollie, Authorize.net, Adyen) without customization.
Flexible Payment Terms
  • Define flexible contract payment schedules tailored to tenant preferences—daily, monthly, quarterly, or yearly options available.
Contract Renewal Options
  • Easily renew or extend your existing contracts online in seconds with just a few simple clicks.
Automated Invoice Generation & Reminders
  • Auto-generate invoices and send reminders in case of missed or upcoming payments.
Email Notifications for Rent Invoices
  • Automatically send email reminders with attached invoices for upcoming rent payments.

Broker / Agent Management

Flexible Commission Setup
  • Support for both fixed and percentage-based commission structures per agent.
Commission History Tracking
  • Access detailed commission records directly within both property listings and individual agent profile pages for transparency.
Dedicated Agent Menu
  • Manage brokers/agents efficiently through a dedicated backend menu.
Commission Frequency
  • Choose to apply commissions one-time or on a recurring monthly basis.
Commission Billing in Odoo
  • Automatically generate commission bills in Odoo's accounting system.

Invoice Management (Installment & Full)

Odoo Invoicing Integration
  • Seamlessly integrated with Odoo’s standard invoicing system for efficient invoice creation and tracking.
Integrated Accounting Management
  • Fully integrated with Odoo Accounting to manage invoices, bills, payments, and financial reports effortlessly.

Property Maintenance Management

Odoo Maintenance App Integration
  • Seamless integration with Odoo Maintenance to manage property maintenance requests and tasks.
Maintenance Billing Automation
  • Automatically generate and manage invoices for property maintenance tasks, integrated with Odoo Accounting.
Team-Based Maintenance Assignment
  • Assign maintenance requests to teams based on expertise, property type, or availability.
Work Hours Tracking
  • Track and log employee work hours on maintenance jobs for better resource planning and billing.

Email Notifications & Auto Reminders

Agreement Email Notifications
  • Automated notifications for Rent and Sale agreement documents with PDF attachments.
Property Booking Alerts
  • Notifications for confirmed and upcoming property bookings to both clients and agents.
Invoice Due Reminders
  • Automated email reminders for upcoming or overdue rental and sales invoice payments.

Property Reports (Sale, Rent & Property Wise)

Excel Reports (XLS)
  • Create comprehensive rent or sales reports for any custom date range you choose.
  • View rent or sale reports grouped by each individual landlord.
PDF Document Reports
  • Create PDF documents such as brochures, booking summaries, and rent or sale agreements with ease.
  • Professional, well-structured documents for all your property needs.

Extra Services, Deposits & Penalties

Additional Services
  • Add optional services such as cleaning, maintenance, or security to rent/lease contracts during creation.
Penalty Invoicing
  • Generate penalty invoices with custom reasons (e.g., late payment, damage) during active rent or lease periods.
Booking Deposit Management
  • Collect and manage booking deposits with proper invoicing and refund tracking for each property.
